About this project

The Business Simulation Project aims to provide an interactive alternative to traditional business case calculations to enhance decision making in the context of business model innovation processes.

It has grown out of a joint research collaboration between the Institute of Technology Management at the University of St. Gallen and BMW, one of the leading automotive companies.
